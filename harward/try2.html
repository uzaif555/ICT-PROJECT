Try a mindfulness exercise today

  1. Sit: Sit on a straight-backed chair or comfortably on the floor.
  2. Focus: Focus on your breath and pay attention to things such as the sensations of air flowing into your nostrils and out of your mouth, or your belly rising and falling as you inhale and exhale.
  3. Expand: Once you've narrowed your concentration in this way, begin to widen your focus. Become aware of sounds, sensations, and ideas.
